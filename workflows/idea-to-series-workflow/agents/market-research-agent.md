# Market Research Agent

## Purpose

Analyze market trends, comp titles, and commercial viability for the author's book/series idea.

## Inputs

- User's book/series idea
- Genre
- Themes

## Outputs

- Trending tropes in the genre
- Comp titles (5-10 similar successful books)
- Market viability score
- Target audience analysis
- Recommendations for positioning

## Skills

Uses market research and trend analysis capabilities.
