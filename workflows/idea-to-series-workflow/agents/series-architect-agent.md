# Series Architect Agent

## Purpose

Design the overall 5-book series structure with escalating stakes, character arcs, and thematic progression.

## Inputs

- Market research results
- User's original idea
- Genre expectations

## Outputs

- 5-book series outline
- Character arcs across all books
- Major plot points for each book
- Thematic progression
- Series hooks and cliffhangers

## Skills

Expert in multi-book series planning and story structure.
